Data Export

OpenDataDiscovery.org is constatly monitoring the status of more than 100 open data portals. The data we collect includes

  • total number of open datasets
  • most used data tags
  • most used data categories
  • organizations that publish most datasets

As this information is collected from the open data community, it will be open to the public. To download the historical data, select a date and click the Download button.
