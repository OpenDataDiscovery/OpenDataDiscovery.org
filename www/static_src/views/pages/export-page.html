Data Export

OpenDataDiscovery.org is constatly monitoring the status of more than 100 open data portals. Every week we record the status of each data portal including

  • total number of open datasets
  • most used data tags
  • most used data categories
  • organizations that publish most datasets

As all information is collected from the open data community, it also belongs to the public. To get a snapshoot of the history, simply select a date and you could download the historical data in JSON format.
